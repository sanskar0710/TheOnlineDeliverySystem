EVO DI-FARA

At The Pizza House, our legacy traces back to the iconic wood-fired techniques of traditional Italian pizzaiolos, combined with local premium ingredients. Every dough is naturally fermented for 48 hours, stretched by hand, topped with organic San Marzano tomato sauce, fresh creamy mozzarella, and baked to blistered perfection in our 900-degree stone ovens. Taste the passion, authenticity, and love baked into every single slice.
